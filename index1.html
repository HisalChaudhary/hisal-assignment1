About Us

Automatic car wash systems are mechanical facilities used to clean vehicles without the need for manual labor. These systems are prevalent in many areas due to their convenience and efficiency.

Our Services

Basic Wash

This typically includes a pre-soak, high-pressure wash, rinse, and spot-free rinse. It's the most basic and economical option for customers who want a quick exterior clean.

Express Wash

This service is designed for customers who are in a hurry and need a quick exterior clean. It typically includes a condensed version of the basic wash process, focusing on the essential steps to get the vehicle clean quickly.

Full Service Wash

Some automatic car wash facilities offer full-service options where attendants manually prep the vehicle before it goes through the automated wash process. This may include hand pre-washing, spot cleaning, bug and tar removal, and other additional services tailored to the customer's needs.
